Connecting the world one picture at a time

This website was first started on June 20th, 2017 and is the first legit website created by Kris Amerman. It was made in order to practice coding with HTML and CSS.

However, this website's primary objective is to showcase pictures of places around the world in an attempt to better connect people culturally. Additionally, the pictures on this website and the information provided about them aim to make the subject of geography more interesting and inviting to people worldwide.
